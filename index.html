About me

I was born in Oregon and after highschool I joined the Army as a 12S (Geospatial Engineer). I served in the Army as a surveyor from 2007-2015. I was deployed to Iraq in 2009 and Kuwait in support of Operation Enduring Freedom (OEF) in 2014. I attended the United States Drill Sergeant Academy in 2015 and was assigned to an initial training battalion in Fort Leonard Wood, MO. In 2016 I exited the Army and moved to the Greater Seattle area and currently work as a Land surveyor at LDC in Woodinville, WA.


I am a married to my lovely wife and we have two children ages 2 and 6. In my spare time I love to be with my family doing activities and making memories. I also like playing video games, chess, coding, competing in combative tournaments and watching movies. I have been intrigued by coding since 2016 and tried to teach myself with little success. I wanted to go back to school to finish my associates degree but it just seemed boring and unrelated to my current career. So I decided to join UW coding boot camp in hopes of a possible career change.
